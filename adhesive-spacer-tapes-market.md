<p><p>The adhesive spacer tapes market is primarily segmented into two types: double-sided and single-sided tapes. Double-sided tapes feature adhesive on both sides, allowing for bonding between two surfaces, making them ideal for applications like mounting and assembly. In contrast, single-sided tapes have adhesive on only one side, suitable for applications that require a secure bond with a single surface, such as sealing and insulation. Both types offer varied thicknesses and materials to cater to diverse industrial needs and demands.</p></p>
