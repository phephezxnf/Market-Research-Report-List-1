<p><p>The prebiotics market comprises various types, each offering unique benefits. Fructo-oligosaccharides (FOS) promote beneficial gut bacteria, while galacto-oligosaccharides (GOS) support immune health. Synanthrin is known for enhancing mineral absorption. Polydextrose acts as a low-calorie fiber with digestive health benefits, while isomaltooligosaccharide (IMO) aids in digestion and promotes weight management. Resistant dextrin is a soluble fiber that improves gut health and regulates blood sugar levels, making these prebiotics essential for functional foods and dietary supplements.</p></p>
