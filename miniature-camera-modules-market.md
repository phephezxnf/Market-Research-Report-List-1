<p><p>The miniature camera modules market primarily comprises CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) imaging technologies. CCD sensors are known for their high-quality images and excellent low-light performance, making them suitable for applications like professional photography and medical imaging. In contrast, CMOS sensors offer lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them ideal for consumer electronics and mobile devices. The choice between these technologies largely depends on performance requirements and application-specific needs.</p></p>
