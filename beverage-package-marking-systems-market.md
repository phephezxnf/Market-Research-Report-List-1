<p><p>Beverage package marking systems encompass various technologies used to print essential information on packaging. Laser marking utilizes focused light beams to etch permanent codes, providing precision and durability without inks, making it ideal for long-lasting labels. Other methods may include thermal transfer and embossing, catering to specific packaging needs. Each technology plays a crucial role in enhancing product traceability and compliance within the beverage industry.</p></p>
