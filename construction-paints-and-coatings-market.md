<p><p>The construction paints and coatings market is broadly categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based paints use water as a solvent, making them eco-friendly, low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and easy to clean. They are ideal for interior applications and environments prioritizing sustainability. Solvent-based paints use chemical solvents, offering superior durability, adhesion, and resistance to harsh conditions, making them suitable for exterior and industrial use. Both types cater to diverse construction needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and application requirements.</p></p>
