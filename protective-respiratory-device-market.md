<p><p>The protective respiratory device market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on Air Purifying Respirators (APR) and Supplied Air Respirators. APRs filter out contaminants from the air, using materials like filters and cartridges to purify inhaled air, making them suitable for environments with specific airborne hazards. In contrast, Supplied Air Respirators deliver clean, breathable air from a compressed air source, ensuring safety in highly contaminated or low-oxygen environments. Together, these devices play a crucial role in worker protection across diverse industries.</p></p>
