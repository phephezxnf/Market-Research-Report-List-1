<p><p>The medical-grade elastomeric materials market comprises two primary types: thermoset and thermoplastic elastomeric materials. Thermoset elastomers are cross-linked polymers that provide excellent elasticity and stability, making them suitable for applications like seals and molded parts. In contrast, thermoplastic elastomers are versatile materials that can be processed as plastics, offering flexibility and ease of manufacturing. Both types are essential for medical devices and applications, ensuring biocompatibility, durability, and performance in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
