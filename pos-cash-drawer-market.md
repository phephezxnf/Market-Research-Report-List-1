<p><strong>The POS Cash Drawer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Printer-Driven Cash Drawer</li><li>Ethernet-Driven Cash Drawer</li><li>Serial And USB-Driven Cash Drawer</li><li>Manually-Driven Cash Drawer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The POS cash drawer market comprises several types based on connectivity and operation. Printer-driven cash drawers connect directly to receipt printers, automatically opening with sales transactions. Ethernet-driven cash drawers utilize network connections for remote access, enhancing versatility in multi-terminal setups. Serial and USB-driven cash drawers connect to computers via serial ports or USB interfaces, facilitating direct control. Manually-driven cash drawers require physical operation, providing a simple, low-tech solution for cash handling in less automated environments.</p></p>
