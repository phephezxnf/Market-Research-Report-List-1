<p><p>Inverter systems are categorized primarily into three market types: Square Wave Inverters, Modified Sine Wave Inverters, and Pure Sine Wave Inverters. Square Wave Inverters generate a basic square wave output, primarily used for simple loads. Modified Sine Wave Inverters provide a stepped approximation of a sine wave, making them suitable for a broader range of appliances but with limited compatibility. Pure Sine Wave Inverters deliver a smooth sine wave, ideal for sensitive electronics, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency across diverse applications.</p></p>
