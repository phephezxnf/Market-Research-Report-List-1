<p><p>The ice melter market is primarily divided into two types: liquid ice melters and solid ice melters. Liquid ice melters typically consist of chemical solutions that quickly penetrate ice, providing effective melting with faster application. They are ideal for high-traffic areas. Solid ice melters, on the other hand, come in granular forms and dissolve slowly, which allows for prolonged melting action and is often more economical for larger surfaces. Both types cater to different needs based on application speed and efficiency.</p></p>
