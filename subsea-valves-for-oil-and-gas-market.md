<p><p>Subsea valves in the oil and gas market encompass several types, each designed for specific functions. Subsea ball valves offer quick shut-off capabilities and reliable sealing. Subsea check valves prevent backflow and ensure fluid flow in one direction. Subsea gate valves provide minimal flow resistance and are used for on-off control in high-pressure applications. Subsea needle valves allow for precise flow regulation, making them suitable for sampling and control processes. Each type plays a crucial role in subsea operations, enhancing system safety and efficiency.</p></p>
