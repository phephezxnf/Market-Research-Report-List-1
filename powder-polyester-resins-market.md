<p><p>The powder polyester resins market can be categorized primarily into hydroxyl terminated and carboxyl terminated types. Hydroxyl terminated resins offer excellent adhesion and flexibility, making them suitable for coatings and adhesives. In contrast, carboxyl terminated resins provide improved chemical resistance and hardness, often utilized in applications requiring enhanced durability, such as automotive and industrial coatings. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, catering to specific performance requirements and enhancing the properties of end products.</p></p>
