<p><p>Playground artificial grass turf is a synthetic surface designed to replicate natural grass, offering a durable, low-maintenance, and safe alternative for playgrounds. It is made from synthetic fibers like polyethylene or polypropylene, combined with an infill material to provide cushioning and stability. This turf is widely used in schools, parks, daycare centers, and residential playgrounds due to its ability to withstand heavy foot traffic, resist wear and tear, and provide a consistent playing surface year-round. It also reduces the risk of injuries by offering better shock absorption compared to natural grass or hard surfaces.</p><p>The Playground Artificial Grass Turf Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for safe and sustainable playground surfaces.
