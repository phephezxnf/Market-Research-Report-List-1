<p><p>The Community Antenna Television (CATV) amplifiers market comprises two main types: outdoor and indoor CATV amplifiers. Outdoor CATV amplifiers are designed for installation outside buildings to boost signal strength across larger distances, compensating for signal loss due to cable length or environmental factors. Indoor CATV amplifiers, on the other hand, are used within homes or small offices to enhance weak signals received from outdoor installations, ensuring optimal picture quality and reliable reception for users in confined spaces.</p></p>
