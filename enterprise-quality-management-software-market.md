<p><p>Enterprise Quality Management Software (EQMS) can be categorized into three main types: Cloud-Based, Web-Based, and On-Premises. Cloud-Based EQMS offers flexibility and scalability through online access, allowing users to manage quality processes from anywhere, often with lower upfront costs. Web-Based solutions, while similar, may require internet access for full functionality and are often subscription-based. On-Premises EQMS is installed locally on company servers, providing greater control and security, but typically involves higher upfront investments and maintenance responsibilities.</p></p>
