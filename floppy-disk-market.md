<p><p>The floppy disk market comprises several types, primarily categorized by storage capacity. The 160KB floppy disk, an early format, was commonly used for data storage in early personal computers. The 320KB disk, a double-sided version, offered increased capacity, facilitating more extensive data transfer and software storage. Each type served different technological needs and gradually declined with the advent of modern storage solutions.</p></p>
