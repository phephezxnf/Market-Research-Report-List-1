<p><p>The photovoltaic greenhouse market is categorized into single pitch and multiple pitch designs. Single pitch greenhouses have a slanted roof, optimizing solar panel exposure while ensuring efficient light distribution for plants. They are simpler and often more economical to construct. In contrast, multiple pitch greenhouses feature roofs with several slopes, maximizing solar energy capture and providing better ventilation options. This design accommodates larger installations and diverse crops, making it suitable for larger operations focusing on extensive agricultural production alongside energy generation.</p></p>
