<p><strong>The Wireless Video Surveillance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Microwave Transmission</li><li>Digital Microwave Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless video surveillance market is primarily categorized into two types: Analog Microwave Transmission and Digital Microwave Transmission. </p><p>Analog Microwave Transmission relies on traditional analog signals, providing a reliable, low-cost solution for transmitting video footage over short to moderate distances, though it may have limitations in image quality. In contrast, Digital Microwave Transmission uses advanced digital signals, offering superior video quality, enhanced encryption, and greater range. This type is increasingly preferred for its ability to support high-definition video and more robust security features, making it ideal for modern surveillance applications.</p></p>
