<p><strong>The Underfill Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capillary Underfill Material (CUF)</li><li>No Flow Underfill Material (NUF)</li><li>Molded Underfill Material (MUF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfill materials are essential in semiconductor packaging, enhancing durability and performance. The Capillary Underfill Material (CUF) flows into the gap between the chip and substrate through capillary action, providing excellent thermal conductivity. No Flow Underfill Material (NUF) is applied as a paste and doesn’t require flowing, offering ease of use and consistency. Molded Underfill Material (MUF) comes pre-molded, ensuring precise application and uniformity. Each type addresses specific application needs, contributing to improved reliability in electronic devices.</p></p>
