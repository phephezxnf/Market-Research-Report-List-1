<p><p>Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD) machines are classified into two primary market types based on their operation method: power type and gravity type. Power type machines utilize electrical pumps to facilitate the fluid exchange process, offering precision and efficiency in treatment. In contrast, gravity type machines rely on the gravitational force to move dialysate, making them simpler and more cost-effective but less controllable. Both types cater to patients' needs, enhancing the accessibility of home dialysis options.</p></p>
