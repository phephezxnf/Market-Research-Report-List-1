<p><p>The high-purity SiC parts market encompasses three primary types: LPCVD (Low-Pressure Chemical Vapor Deposition) SiC, CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) SiC, and High-Density Sintered SiC. LPCVD SiC offers excellent uniformity and low defects, making it ideal for semiconductor applications. CVD SiC, known for its high-quality crystalline structures, is utilized in advanced electronics and photonics. High-Density Sintered SiC is favored for its mechanical strength and thermal stability, suitable for high-performance applications in industries such as aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
