<p><strong>The Friction Welding Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Friction Welding</li><li>Linear Friction Welding</li><li>Friction Stir Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Friction welding equipment encompasses three main types: Rotary Friction Welding, Linear Friction Welding, and Friction Stir Welding. Rotary Friction Welding involves the rotation of one workpiece against a stationary piece, generating heat through friction to create a joint. Linear Friction Welding uses a back-and-forth motion to produce similar results, suitable for small parts. Friction Stir Welding employs a rotating tool to soften materials without melting, ideal for aluminum and similar alloys. Each method offers unique benefits for specific applications.</p></p>
