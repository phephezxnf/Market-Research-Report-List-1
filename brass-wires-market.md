<p><ul><li>Alpha Brass</li><li>Alpha-beta Brass</li><li>Beta Brass</li><li>Gamma Brass</li><li>White Brass Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brass wires market comprises several key types, each distinguished by their alloy compositions. Alpha Brass features high ductility, ideal for cold working. Alpha-beta Brass, combining properties of both, offers enhanced strength and corrosion resistance. Beta Brass is known for its high strength and lower ductility, suitable for applications requiring durability. Gamma Brass contains a higher zinc content, enhancing strength and brittleness. White Brass, with additional nickel, provides excellent corrosion resistance, making it suitable for specialized applications.</p></p>
