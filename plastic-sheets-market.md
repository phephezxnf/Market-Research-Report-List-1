<p><p>The plastic sheets market comprises various types, each serving different applications. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) is valued for its flexibility and strength, while LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) offers excellent transparency and chemical resistance. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is known for sturdiness and durability. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) features enhanced clarity and moisture barrier properties. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) presents excellent sealing capabilities. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile with good chemical resistance. Other materials may include PET, PS, and specific engineered plastics, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
