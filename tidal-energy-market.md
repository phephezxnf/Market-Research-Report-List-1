<p><p>Tidal energy markets consist of various technologies harnessing oceanic tides for power generation. Tidal stream generators utilize underwater turbines to capture kinetic energy from moving water. Pendulum devices function similarly, transforming wave motion into energy through mechanical oscillation. Barrages are large structures that create a difference in water levels between tidal cycles, driving turbines for electricity generation. Other innovative approaches include dynamic tidal power and hybrid systems, which integrate tidal energy with other renewable sources for enhanced efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
