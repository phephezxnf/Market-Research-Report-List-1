<p><p>The Quick Lime market is primarily divided into two types: High Calcium Quicklime and Dolomitic Quicklime. High Calcium Quicklime, composed mainly of calcium oxide, is used in industries like steel, water treatment, and environmental applications, offering high purity. Dolomitic Quicklime contains both calcium and magnesium oxides, providing enhanced properties for specific applications, such as in the construction and agricultural sectors. Both types play crucial roles in chemical processes, pH regulation, and material production, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
