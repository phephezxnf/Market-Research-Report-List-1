<p><ul><li>Photon Detection</li><li>Thermal Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thermal IR Sensors market is divided into two primary types: photon detection and thermal detection. Photon detection sensors operate by detecting individual photons and are highly sensitive, ideal for low-temperature environments and applications like gas analysis or high-resolution imaging. In contrast, thermal detection sensors measure the temperature difference based on thermal radiation emitted by objects, making them suitable for applications such as surveillance and industrial monitoring. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to varying sensitivity and detection needs.</p></p>
