<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Hair Dryer</li><li>Axial Hair Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wall-mounted hair dryer market includes two primary types: centrifugal and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers utilize a fan to draw air in and expel it at high speeds, offering powerful drying capabilities suitable for high-traffic areas like salons and gyms. Axial hair dryers, on the other hand, feature a blade design to push air directly out, providing a smoother airflow ideal for personal bathrooms or hotels, where a quieter and more efficient drying experience is preferred.</p></p>
