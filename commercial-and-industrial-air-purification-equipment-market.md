<p><p>The commercial and industrial air purification equipment market encompasses various systems designed to improve air quality. Dust collectors capture airborne particulates, essential for maintaining clean work environments. Mist collectors remove liquid aerosols from the air, crucial in industries like manufacturing. HEPA filters provide high-efficiency particulate air filtration, removing tiny particles for improved indoor air quality. Baghouse filters utilize cloth bags to separate dust from gases, commonly used in heavy industries. Other systems might include electrostatic precipitators or activated carbon filters, addressing specific air quality needs.</p></p>
