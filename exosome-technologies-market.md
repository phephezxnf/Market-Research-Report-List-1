Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis (NTA) quantifies the concentration and size distribution of exosomes in liquid samples, providing dynamic assessments. Western Blot Molecular Marker Detection enables the identification of specific proteins associated with exosomes, facilitating functional analyses. Together, these techniques are crucial for advancing research and diagnostics in fields like cancer, neurodegenerative diseases, and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
