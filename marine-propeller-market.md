<p><strong>The Marine Propeller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Controllable Pitch Propeller</li><li>Fixed Pitch Propeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine propeller market comprises two main types: controllable pitch propellers (CPP) and fixed pitch propellers (FPP). CPP allows for dynamic adjustment of blade angles, enabling optimized performance for varying speeds and conditions, enhancing fuel efficiency and maneuverability. In contrast, FPP has a fixed blade angle, providing simplicity and reliability, often preferred for steady-speed applications. Both types cater to diverse marine vessels, influencing operational efficiency, maintenance, and overall maritime performance requirements.</p></p>
