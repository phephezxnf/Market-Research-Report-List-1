<p><p>The anthracite market is categorized primarily into lump anthracite and anthracite fines. Lump anthracite consists of larger, visually appealing coal pieces, ideal for heating applications and industrial use due to its high carbon content and low impurities. In contrast, anthracite fines are smaller particles resulting from mining and handling processes, often used for various applications, including filtration, cement production, and as a component in some energy processes. Both types serve distinct market needs based on their size and application.</p></p>
