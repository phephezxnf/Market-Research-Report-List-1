<p><p>The Smart Switchable Glass market includes various technologies that enable glass to change transparency and light transmission properties. Electrochromic (EC) glass adjusts its tint through electrical signals, providing energy efficiency. Suspended Particle Devices (SPD) glass contains particles that align with an electric current, controlling light passage. Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) glass utilizes liquid crystals that switch from opaque to transparent when voltage is applied. Other types may include advancements in smart coatings and technologies enhancing energy efficiency and privacy.</p></p>
