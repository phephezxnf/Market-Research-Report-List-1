<p><p>The PTFE anti-corrosion coatings market is segmented into powder and liquid coatings. Powder coatings involve a dry powder applied electrostatically, creating a durable finish when cured, ideal for large surfaces and high wear resistance. Liquid coatings, on the other hand, are applied using brushes, rollers, or spray techniques, offering uniform coverage and flexibility for intricate shapes. Both types provide excellent chemical resistance, reducing corrosion risk on various substrates, and are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
