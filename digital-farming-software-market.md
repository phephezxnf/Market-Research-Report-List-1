<p><ul><li>Local/Web-Based</li><li>Cloud-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital farming software is categorized into two primary types: local/web-based and cloud-based. Local/web-based software operates on individual devices or a network, allowing farmers to manage their operations directly from their computers. In contrast, cloud-based software stores data online, enabling farmers to access information from anywhere with an internet connection. Cloud solutions typically offer real-time data sharing and collaboration features, enhancing operational efficiency and decision-making, while local systems may provide greater control over sensitive data and require limited internet access.</p></p>
