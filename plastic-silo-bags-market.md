<p><p>Plastic silo bags are primarily made from polyethylene (PE) and polypropylene (PP), each offering distinct benefits for agricultural storage. PE bags are known for their flexibility, good moisture resistance, and UV protection, making them ideal for long-term storage of grains and silage. In contrast, PP bags provide increased strength, durability, and resistance to tearing and punctures, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both materials contribute to efficient storage solutions, ensuring high-quality preservation of agricultural products while minimizing spoilage and waste.</p></p>
