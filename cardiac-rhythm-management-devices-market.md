<p><p>The cardiac rhythm management devices market primarily includes implantable defibrillators and bi-ventricular pacemakers. Implantable defibrillators automatically monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks when dangerous arrhythmias occur, aiding in sudden cardiac arrest prevention. Bi-ventricular pacemakers, on the other hand, are used to treat heart failure by coordinating the contractions of the heart's ventricles, enhancing overall efficiency and improving symptoms. Together, these devices help manage complex heart conditions, improving patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
