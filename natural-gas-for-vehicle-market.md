<p><p>The Natural Gas for Vehicle (NGV) market primarily includes Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) and Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG). CNG is natural gas compressed to less than 1% of its volume at standard pressure, stored in high-pressure tanks, and used in light-duty vehicles like cars and buses. LNG is natural gas cooled to -162°C, liquefied for higher energy density, and used in heavy-duty vehicles like trucks and ships. Both are cleaner alternatives to gasoline and diesel, reducing emissions and operational costs. CNG dominates urban transport, while LNG suits long-haul logistics.</p></p>
