<p><p>The Smart Glass Partition market includes Electrochromic, Thermochromic, and Photochromic types. Electrochromic partitions use electric voltage to change transparency, offering energy efficiency and privacy control. Thermochromic partitions respond to temperature changes, automatically adjusting opacity to regulate heat and light. Photochromic partitions react to UV light, darkening in bright conditions to reduce glare and UV exposure. Each type enhances modern spaces by combining functionality, aesthetics, and sustainability, catering to diverse applications in offices, homes, and commercial settings.</p></p>
