<p><ul><li>Nylon 6</li><li>Nylon 66</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nylon Cord Fabric Market comprises two primary types: Nylon 6 and Nylon 66. Nylon 6 is produced through a process called ring-opening polymerization, offering excellent elasticity and resilience, making it ideal for applications like automotive tires and industrial fabrics. Nylon 66, on the other hand, is derived from a condensation polymerization process, providing superior strength and wear resistance, often used in high-performance applications such as sporting goods and heavy-duty textiles. Both types cater to diverse industries, driving market growth.</p></p>
