<p><p>The emissions control catalyst market encompasses three main types: honeycomb catalysts, plate catalysts, and corrugated catalysts. Honeycomb catalysts feature a porous, honeycomb structure that maximizes surface area for effective catalytic reactions, commonly used in automotive applications. Plate catalysts have flat surfaces, suitable for specific industrial processes, offering efficient emissions reduction. Corrugated catalysts utilize a wavy design that enhances flow dynamics while maintaining compactness, making them ideal for various engineering applications. Each type serves distinct needs in reducing harmful emissions.</p></p>
