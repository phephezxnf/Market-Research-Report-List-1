<p><ul><li>Lift Pump</li><li>Force Pump</li><li>Axial Piston Pump</li><li>Radial Piston Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piston pump market encompasses various types, including lift pumps, force pumps, axial piston pumps, and radial piston pumps. Lift pumps use suction to raise fluids, while force pumps operate under pressure to push fluids through a system. Axial piston pumps feature pistons aligned parallel to the drive shaft, optimizing efficiency in high-pressure applications. In contrast, radial piston pumps have pistons arranged radially, offering high torque and power density. Each type serves distinct applications across sectors like agriculture, manufacturing, and hydraulics.</p></p>
