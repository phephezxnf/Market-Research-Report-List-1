<p><p>The High Purity PC market is characterized by two primary production methods: the Phosgene Interface Method and the Melt Transesterification Method. The Phosgene Interface Method involves the reaction of phenol and phosgene, resulting in high-purity polycarbonate with excellent optical and thermal properties, ideal for demanding applications. Conversely, the Melt Transesterification Method utilizes phenol and dimethyl carbonate, offering a more environmentally friendly alternative. Both methods cater to industries like automotive, electronics, and medical, emphasizing sustainability and product quality.</p></p>
