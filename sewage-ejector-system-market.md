<p><ul><li>Submersible</li><li>Vertical</li><li>Self-priming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sewage ejector system market is categorized into three main types: submersible, vertical, and self-priming systems. Submersible ejectors are installed underwater, effectively handling wastewater by minimizing noise and vibration. Vertical ejectors are positioned above the sewage level, making maintenance easier, while effectively lifting waste vertically. Self-priming systems draw wastewater efficiently from lower levels without requiring priming pumps, making them ideal for applications where flooding is a concern. Each type caters to specific installation needs and spatial constraints, ensuring reliable wastewater management.</p></p>
