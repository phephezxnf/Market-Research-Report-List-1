<p><p>The ultrafast terahertz sensors market is categorized into two main types: photonic sensing and electronic sensing. Photonic sensing utilizes light-based techniques to detect terahertz radiation, offering advantages like high sensitivity and real-time measurement, making it ideal for applications in imaging and spectroscopy. Electronic sensing, on the other hand, relies on traditional electronic devices to capture terahertz signals, providing robustness and integration with existing electronic systems. Both types are crucial for advancements in telecommunications, security, and material characterization.</p></p>
