<p><p>The HiFi headphone market is categorized into three main types: in-ear, on-ear, and over-ear headphones. In-ear headphones are compact and fit snugly within the ear canal, providing portability and passive noise isolation. On-ear headphones rest on the ears, offering a balance between sound quality and comfort but may allow some external noise. Over-ear headphones encase the ears completely, delivering superior sound quality and comfort but are bulkier. Each type caters to different user preferences for sound experience and convenience.</p></p>
