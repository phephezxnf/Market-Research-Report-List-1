<p><p>Air curtains and unit heaters are essential components in climate control systems, categorized into recirculating and non-recirculating markets. Recirculating air curtains utilize existing indoor air to create a barrier, enhancing energy efficiency and maintaining indoor temperatures while allowing easy passage through doorways. Non-recirculating air curtains, on the other hand, draw in fresh air, providing a more direct approach to temperature control. Unit heaters, used for localized heating, can also adopt these types, catering to different environmental needs and energy efficiency goals.</p></p>
