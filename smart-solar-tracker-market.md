<p><p>The Smart Solar Tracker market is divided into two main types: Single Axis and Dual Axis. Single Axis trackers rotate on one axis, typically aligning with the sun's east-west movement, maximizing energy capture throughout the day. In contrast, Dual Axis trackers adjust on two axes, allowing for tracking both the sun’s daily path and seasonal variations, optimizing solar energy absorption. This versatility in tracking capabilities enhances overall efficiency, catering to diverse solar installation needs and geographical conditions.</p></p>
