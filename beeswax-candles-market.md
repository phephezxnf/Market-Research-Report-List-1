<p><strong>The Beeswax Candles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure Beeswax</li><li>Mixed Beeswax</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beeswax candles market is primarily divided into two types: pure beeswax and mixed beeswax. Pure beeswax candles are made entirely from natural honeycomb wax, offering a longer burn time and a natural, pleasant aroma. They are often favored for their eco-friendly properties and air-purifying qualities. In contrast, mixed beeswax candles combine beeswax with other waxes, such as paraffin or soy, making them more affordable and accessible while potentially sacrificing some of the natural benefits associated with pure beeswax.</p></p>
