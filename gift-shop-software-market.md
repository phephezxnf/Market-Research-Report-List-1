<p><p>Gift shop software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, enabling easy access from any device with internet connectivity. This model offers flexibility, automatic updates, and scalability, making it ideal for small to medium-sized shops. In contrast, on-premise software is installed locally on the shop's computers, providing more control over data and customization. However, it requires upfront investment and ongoing maintenance, making it suitable for larger operations with specific needs.</p></p>
