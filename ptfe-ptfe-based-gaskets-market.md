<p><p>PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is a versatile, chemically resistant polymer used in various applications, particularly in gaskets. The PTFE-based gasket market includes three key types: </p><p>1. **Virgin PTFE**: Pure, unmodified PTFE providing excellent chemical resistance and low friction.</p><p>2. **Filled PTFE**: PTFE combined with fillers like glass or carbon to enhance strength and thermal conductivity. </p><p>3. **Expanded PTFE**: A lightweight, porous form that offers enhanced sealing performance and compressibility, ideal for irregular surfaces. Each type serves specific industrial needs based on their properties.</p></p>
