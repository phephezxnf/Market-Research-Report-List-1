<p><p>The solar tile market primarily comprises two types: monocrystalline and polycrystalline tiles. Monocrystalline tiles are made from a single crystal structure, offering higher efficiency and a sleek design, making them ideal for residential applications where space is limited. Conversely, polycrystalline tiles consist of multiple crystal fragments, generally providing lower efficiency but at a more affordable price. Both types serve the growing demand for solar energy solutions, catering to various consumer preferences and budget considerations, thus shaping the overall market landscape.</p></p>
