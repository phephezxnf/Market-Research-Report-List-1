<p><strong>The VoLTE for Communication Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voice Over IP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS)</li><li>Circuit Switched Fall Back (CSFB)</li><li>Dual Radio/Simultaneous Voice and LTE (SVLTE)</li><li>Voice Over LTE Via Generic Access Network (VOLGA)</li><li>Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VoLTE enhances communication by utilizing various market types. Voice Over IP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS) integrates voice and multimedia services over the LTE network. Circuit Switched Fall Back (CSFB) allows calls to switch to circuit-switched networks when necessary. Dual Radio/Simultaneous Voice and LTE (SVLTE) enables simultaneous voice and data transmission. Voice Over LTE Via Generic Access Network (VOLGA) supports voice services using generic access protocols. Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC) ensures seamless call transfer between LTE and legacy networks.</p></p>
