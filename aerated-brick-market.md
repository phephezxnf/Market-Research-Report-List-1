<p><p>Aerated bricks, also known as autoclaved aerated concrete (AAC) blocks, are lightweight, precast building materials made from a mixture of sand, cement, lime, water, and an expanding agent like aluminum powder. These bricks are known for their excellent thermal insulation, fire resistance, and soundproofing properties, making them a popular choice in modern construction. They are eco-friendly, as they utilize industrial waste materials and require less energy during production compared to traditional bricks.</p><p>The Aerated Brick Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for sustainable and energy-efficient construction materials. These factors collectively contribute to the robust growth trajectory of the aerated brick market.</p></p>
