<p><strong>The Defibrillator Pads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual External</li><li>Manual Internal</li><li>Automatic External</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Defibrillator pads are essential in resuscitation, available in three primary types. Manual external pads are used with handheld defibrillators, allowing operators to deliver shocks based on real-time assessment. Manual internal pads are utilized by healthcare professionals during surgeries, directly placed on the heart to restore rhythm. Automatic external defibrillator (AED) pads are designed for public use, providing voice prompts and automatic shock delivery, making them user-friendly for untrained bystanders in emergencies. Each type serves distinct clinical and emergency situations.</p></p>
