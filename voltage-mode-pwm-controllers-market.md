<p><p>Voltage Mode PWM controllers are essential in power management, utilizing Pulse Width Modulation to regulate output voltage. The market is segmented into Full-Bridge and Half-Bridge PWM controllers. Full-Bridge PWM controllers use four switching devices, providing efficient power conversion for high output applications like industrial and renewable energy systems. In contrast, Half-Bridge PWM controllers utilize two switches to deliver moderate power efficiently, commonly employed in lower-power applications such as consumer electronics. Both types cater to diverse energy efficiency and performance needs.</p></p>
