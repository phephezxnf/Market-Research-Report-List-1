<p><strong>The Fusion Splicer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core Alignment</li><li>Cladding Alignment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fusion splicer market can be categorized primarily into two types: core alignment and cladding alignment. Core alignment splicers ensure precise joining of the optical fibers' cores, delivering reduced splice loss and higher performance, making them ideal for high-capacity networks. In contrast, cladding alignment splicers align the cladding layers of the fibers, offering a simpler and more cost-effective solution, suitable for less demanding applications. Both types cater to different requirements in the telecommunications and data transmission sectors.</p></p>
