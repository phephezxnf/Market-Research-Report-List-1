<p><p>The gas cell market comprises three main types: open, sealed, and vapor markets. Open gas cells allow gas exchange with the atmosphere, enabling direct measurement of ambient conditions. Sealed gas cells are closed systems that contain a fixed amount of gas, ensuring stable conditions for accurate analysis over time. Vapor markets involve the manipulation and measurement of gas vapor in specialized environments, focusing on applications like sensors and chemical reactions. Each type serves different analytical and industrial needs.</p></p>
